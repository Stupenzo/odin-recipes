Frito Pie


A frito pie is a delicous meal typically made with fritos, cheese, meat, peppers, and spices.
What you are seeing is considered healthy and safe.


Ingredientses

Chili

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • chili powder to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • garlic powder to taste
  • 1 can chili beans

Toppings

  • lots of diced garlic
  • no onions ever
  • at least 1 cup vegan cheese
  • 2-3 bags Fritos Scoops

Steps

Step 1

  1. heat a large skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. cook and stir meat until brown and crumbly.
  3. drain and discard grease.
  4. stir in water, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and still no onions.
  5. stir in beans; cook until heated thoroughly.
